FuRead

Bringing modern Android apps to your e-reader.

What is FuRead?

FuRead is an open-source initiative focused on adapting and optimizing contemporary Android applications for the unique constraints and display characteristics of Android-based e-readers (like Onyx Boox, Boyue Likebook, etc.).

Our goal is to make your favorite modern apps usable and enjoyable on your e-ink device, preserving battery life and leveraging the paper-like display.

Features & Goals

  • App compatibility analysis and patching.
  • UI optimization for e-ink screens (contrast, refresh rates).
  • Battery life improvements for ported apps.
  • Community-driven development and testing.
  • Guides and tutorials for users.
